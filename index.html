The Straw Hats

Captain of the Straw Hats: Monkey D. Luffy (Current Bounty:$1,500,000,000)

The protagonist of the show is Monkey D. Luffy and he is the captain of "Straw Hat Pirates".
Luffy and his crew are called the "Straw Hats" because of Luffy's signiture Straw Hat. His
straw hat was given to him by a great pirate named Shanks. When Shanks gave Luffy his
treasured hat, Luffy declared that he would be King of the Pirates! Luffy ate the Gum Gum
fruit which turned him into a rubber man. This allows him to stretch and fight in a really
unique way!


Capain's Right Hand Man: Roronoa Zoro (Current Bounty:$320,000,000)

The first person that Luffy asked to join his crew was "Pirate Hunter" Zoro. Zoro is a master
swordsman that uses three swords at once. He made a promise to his childhood friend that they
would become the world's strongest swordsmen. Shortly after they made this promise Zoro's
childhood friend died. Zoro's undying loyalty to his captain and his dream make him a force
to be reckoned with.


Navigator of the Straw Hats: Nami (Current Bounty:$66,000,000)

Second person to be recruited by Luffy. "Cat Burgler" Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hats
and she is an expert navigator and her dream is to one day draw a map of the entire world. Her
her town was taken over by fishmen, these fishmen ruled with an ironfist demanding everyone
pay an insane amount of money monthly per person. If they did not pay they were killed.
Nami was adopted by a woman named Bellemere. This family consisted of herself, Bellemere,
and another adopted child named Nojiko. They did not have enough money for all of them so
Bellemere sacrificed herself by paying for her children. Luffy and his crew came along years
later and defeated the fishmen enslaving their town, freeing them all.


Sniper of the Staw Hats: GOD Usopp (Current Bounty:$200,000,000)

The fourth member of the Straw Hats God Usopp. Usopp is a master sniper with deadly accuracy.
Usopp being one of the weakest members of the group uses tricks against his opponents and is
cowardly. Usopp's dad left his family to the sea to become a pirate which resulted in him lying
about pirate's invading his town. When pirates eventually did invade his town no one believed
him, the only people that believed him were Luffy and his crew. The Straw Hats saved the town
and then Usopp was planning on becoming a pirate but Luffy invited him to join his crew.


Cook of the Straw Hats: Vinsmoke Sanji (Current Bounty:$330,000,000)

The fifth member of the Straw Hats,"Black Leg" Vinsmoke Sanji, is an expert cook and fighter.
Sanji's fighting style consists of only kicks, he does not use his hands to fight because his hands
are for cooking food.


Ship Doctor of the Straw Hats: Tony Tony Chopper (Current Bounty:$100)

The sixth member of the Straw Hats,"Cotton Candy Lover" Chopper, is a an expert doctor who
is also a reindeer. Chopper was a reindeer and ate a human human fruit and became a mix of the two.


Archeologist/Historian of the Straw Hats: Nico Robin (Current Bounty:$130,000,000)

The Seventh member of the Straw Hats,"Devil Child" Nico Robin, is one of the few that can
decipher and read the ancient "Poneglyphs". These poneglyphs are the key to uncovering the
secret history behind the Void Century. There is another type of poneglyph called the "Road
Poneglyph", these four special poneglyphs are necessary to find the "One Piece" and
becoming Pirate King.


Shipwright of the Straw Hats: Franky (Current Bounty:$94,000,000)

The Eigth member of the Straw Hats,"Cyborg Franky, is a master craftsman and shipwright.
Franky's dream is to build a ship that can sail all across the world. Franky built the "Thousand Sunny"
which replaced the Straw Hats old ship.


Musician of the Straw Hats: Brook (Current Bounty:$83,000,000)

The Ninth member of the Straw Hats,"Soul King" Brook, is musician who came back from
the dead. Brook died but because he ate the Yomi Yomi fruit his soul returned to his body.
However, Brook's soul could not find his body so when he finally found his body, his body
was nothing but bones.


Helmsman of the Straw Hats: Jinbe (Current Bounty:$400,000,000)

The Tenth member of the Straw Hats,"Knight of the Sea" Jinbe, is a fishman who was once a
warlord of the sea. Warlords are pirates that are recognized by the World Governemnt and are
used to deter other pirates. Jinbe only became a warlord of the sea to protect fishman island
from invasion.
